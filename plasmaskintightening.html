FAQ


Our service focuses on a skin care method called Plasma Pro Skin Tightening - using plasma to eliminate excess skin. This treatment is available for eye lift, skin tag removal, tummy tightening, and stretch mark repair without any surgery, scalpel, stitches or bandages and minimal downtime. A minimally invasive procedure with much lower risk and more affordable then actual surgery.

PERMANENT RESULT?

  • The treament result is depended on each person
  • This is not a permanent effects
  • It is recommended to avoid smoking, alcohol consumption, and prolonged sun exposure
  • Race and genetics can also have an effect of the length of beneficial effects

REDUCE THE PAIN

  • Clients should take TYLENOL prior to the appointment but NOT ASPRIN
  • High quality numbing cream will be applied before the treatment
  • It is okay to have a nap during the procedure! :)

ON THE TREATMENT DAY

  • Do not wear any make up on the day of the treatment including false eyelashes
  • Take antihistamines for 2 - 3 days if swelling happens
  • Please be advised that treatment times could take at least two hour

POST TREATMENT ADVICE

  • Follow the skin care instruction
  • Avoid smoking, alcohol and sleep enough
  • Keep dry and avoid touching the treatment area
  • Be advised to wear sun glasses while going out

RESTRICTION

  • Clients should be in good health at the time of the appointment, with no preexisting health conditions
  • Clients with diabetes, healing disorders or lymphatic draining issues should not undergo treatment
  • Treatments are not recommended for people with keloid scars, diebetes, blood and healing disorders, sexually transmitted diseases, pacemakers, or pregnant women
  • Individuals with darker complexions are at risk for hyperpigmentation
  • Clients under any medical or cosmetic treatment need to be rescheduled
